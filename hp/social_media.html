-Social media-

Information and technology has advanced rapidly, our team loves to interact with the society in different ways, in this case through social media. We believe there are ideas worth spreading as we carry out the whole project. As a result, we are active on our Facebook fan page and we also created a YouTube channel to share our thoughts and knowledge to the audience. We try as much as we can to connect our team, our project and our passion to people on social media.

Facebook

    iGEM CSMU X NCHU on Facebook is a page with a community of over a thousand people. We use this platform to share information regarding our project, helping people understand the problem of Aflatoxins in particular, as well as scientific knowledge, trends in the synthetic biology industries, what iGEM is about, our Human Practices, and any updates about our team.

    We hope that this will be able to reach and impact a large audience since this is one of the most used social media platforms. People interact with us through comments and messages. Collaborations and meetups with other iGEM teams are often communicated through Facebook as well. We delivered in both English and Chinese on the page.

Youtube

    Our team has a Youtube channel, delivering information in the form of videos on this global social media platform.

    Through both visual and auditory stimulation, we hope to create engaging videos that would be able to help people understand our project, iGEM, our Human Practices, our team, and some interesting science fun facts.
