General Concern and Regulation of Biosafety

All experiments of our iGEM project were conducted in the Biosafety Level-1 laboratories under the regulation of Environmental Safety and Health Center of Chung Shan Medical University. The emergency exit and basic risk assessment are the first thing to check of everyday prior to the experiment.
